CHARACTER STAT GENERATION RULES:

RARITY:
- 3-star = lower stat budget
- 4-star = medium stat budget
- 5-star = highest stat budget

BASE STAT BUDGET BY RARITY:
- 3-star: 1800 to 2200 total stat points
- 4-star: 2300 to 2800 total stat points
- 5-star: 2900 to 3500 total stat points

STATS TO DISTRIBUTE:
- HP
- ATK
- DEF
- SPEED

*Each level increases HP,ATK,DEF and SPEED by small intervals. store per-level growth values per character.

DISTRIBUTION LOGIC:
- Fast/agile characters get more speed, lower defense
- Tank characters get more HP and defense
- Power characters get more attack
- Lore should influence stat weighting, not just random values

RULE:
- Randomization should stay inside controlled ranges so characters feel unique but still balanced

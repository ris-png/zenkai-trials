- Any secondary effect whose target differs from the main skill target must explicitly define its own target

SKILL EFFECT TYPES:

DAMAGE:
- Single target damage
- Multi-hit attacks
- Area (AOE) damage

STATUS EFFECTS:

Stun:
- Target cannot act on their next turn
- Duration: 1 turn
- Cannot stack

Burn:
- Deals damage over time each turn
- Duration: defined per skill
- Does not stack (refreshes duration if applied again)

Freeze:
- Reduces speed by 30%
- Target acts last in the next round
- Duration: 1 turn
- Cannot stack

Shock:
- Reduces speed by 50%
- Duration: 2 turns
- Does not stack (refreshes duration)

Poison:
- Deals damage over time based on % of max HP
- Duration: defined per skill
- Can stack (damage increases per stack)

DEBUFFS:

ATK Down:
- Reduces attack by a % (defined per skill)
- Duration: defined per skill

DEF Down:
- Reduces defense by a % (defined per skill)
- Duration: defined per skill

SPEED Down:
- Reduces speed by a % (defined per skill)
- Duration: defined per skill

BUFFS:

ATK Up:
- Increases attack by a % (defined per skill)
- Duration: defined per skill

DEF Up:
- Increases defense by a % (defined per skill)
- Duration: defined per skill

SPEED Up:
- Increases speed by a % (defined per skill)
- Duration: defined per skill

UTILITY:

Shield:
- Absorbs incoming damage before HP is reduced
- Value defined per skill
- Expires when broken or duration ends

Lifesteal:
- Heals for a % of damage dealt
- Applied instantly after damage

Energy Drain:
- Reduces enemy mana by a % of their current mana
- Cannot reduce below 0

Energy Boost:
- Restores mana to self or ally
- Cannot exceed max mana

Taunt:
- Forces enemy to target the taunting character
- Duration: 1 turn

Cleanse:
- Removes all negative effects
- Target: single ally or entire team (defined per skill)

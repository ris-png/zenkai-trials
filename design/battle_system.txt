BATTLE SYSTEM:

MATCH FORMAT:
- Player can bring 1 to 3 characters
- Enemy side has 1 boss
- Battle ends when all player characters are defeated or the boss is defeated

TURN ORDER:
- At the start of each round, all living units gain mana
- Then all living units are sorted by speed (highest to lowest)
- If there is a speed tie, player units act before enemy units
- Each living unit takes 1 action per round

ELEMENTS:
- Earth, Water, Fire, Air, Lightning, Light, Dark

ELEMENT RELATIONSHIPS:
- Earth > Water
- Water > Fire
- Fire > Air
- Air > Lightning
- Lightning > Earth
- Light > Dark
- Dark > Light
- Light and Dark are neutral against Earth, Water, Fire, Air, Lightning

ELEMENT MULTIPLIERS:
- Strong = 1.20
- Weak = 0.80
- Neutral = 1.00

ACTIONS:
- Basic Attack: 0 mana
- Skill 1: low mana cost, medium damage
- Skill 2 (Ultimate): high mana cost, high damage
- Dodge: 0 mana, coin flip → success = 0 damage, fail = full damage
- Block: 0 mana → take 20% damage, cannot dodge next turn

MANA:
- Start: 150
- Gain per round: 150
- Max: 600

CRITICAL HIT:
- 3.5% chance
- Critical hit multiplies final damage by 1.50

DAMAGE FORMULA:
Final Damage = Base Damage × Element Multiplier × Crit Modifier × Block Modifier

SPEED:
- Range: 100 to 1000
- Higher speed acts first

PASSIVE ABILITIES:
- Each character has 1 passive ability
- Passives may be battle passives or reward passives. 
Reward passives apply only after battle
- - Battle passives remain active only while their source character is alive
- Passive abilities can affect:
  - Self
  - Team (all allies)
  - Enemy (rare cases)

Examples:
- +5% team attack
- +10% self speed
- +5% team XP gain (xp gain applies post battle)
- Reduce incoming damage by 5%
- Increase crit chance by 2%

SPECIAL RULES:
- Dead characters cannot act
- A blocked character cannot use dodge on the next turn
- Dodge only affects the next incoming damage instance
- Mana cannot exceed max
- Passive effects stack additively unless specified otherwise
